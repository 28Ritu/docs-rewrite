<h3>Introduction</h3>
<h5>Voice is the future.</h5>
How will your customers interact with you in the future? Using voice. How will your customers interact with service agents? Using voice. How will your potential customers find you? Using voice. But the key companies in this space - Google, Amazon, Microsoft and Apple - see voice are building voice on their terms.
<h5>Mycroft is different.</h5>
Mycroft offers a brandable, open source voice stack that can be integrated into your enterprise offering - automotive, elder care or industrial settings. Mycroft is data private and independent from incumbents. The elements of our voice stack - Wake Word, Speech to Text, Skills, Text to Speech and home.mycroft.ai platform - are easily integratable into other offerings.
<h3>Capabilities and use cases</h3>
Mycroft fulfils several use cases.
<h4>Hosted voice assistant</h4>
<ul>
 	<li>Deploy a voice assistant to face your customers hosted on your cloud. Give it a name and voice that matches your brand and deploy it in your hardware, or base a device on our Mark II reference device.</li>
 	<li>Ensure your customer data remains in your enterprise so no one - especially Amazon and Google - know and serve your customers better than you do.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Enterprise voice assistant</h4>
<ul>
 	<li>Use Mycroft to control your corporate campus, retrive information via voice in industrial or operator settings, securely on premises.</li>
 	<li>Use Mycroft with specific enterprise datasets to assist customers in service settings.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Embedded systems</h4>
<ul>
 	<li>Incorporate Mycroft into vehicles to provide the driver or operator with a readily available voice assistant, without taking hands off the wheel.</li>
 	<li>Incorporate Mycroft voice into other embedded systems, such as Internet of Things (IoT)</li>
</ul>
<h4>Customised Skills</h4>
Mycroft Skills are written in <a href="https://www.python.org/">Python</a>, one of the most-used programming languages worldwide. This allows quick and fast customisation of bespoke Skills and voice capabilities for your enterprise. Importantly, Python's capabilities allow fast access to corporate datasets, such as price lists, component information or business rules, easily turning your enterprise's data into valuable voice Skills. Mycroft has a rapidly growing library of open source Skills, which can be used as the basis for further development.

<a href="http://mycroft.ai/documentation/skills/">Learn more about our already-available Skills</a>.
